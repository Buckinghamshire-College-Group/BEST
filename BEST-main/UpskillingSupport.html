Buckinghamshire Council | Skills Bootcamps

Skills Bootcamps are free, practical and engaging short courses helping to equip individuals aged 19+ with the knowledge and skills needed by employers by linking them with line of sight to a job/ different role, additional responsibilities or new opportunities.

The Courses:

  • Cover a variety of skills that are currently in-demand
  • Last up to 16 weeks
  • Are fully funded for the learner.

Jobcentre Plus | Sector Based Work Academy Progrgamme (SWAPs)

SWAPs gives jobseekers who are 16 and over, and claiming benefits, the opportunity to apply for jobs. This programme can last up to 6 weeks and includes:

  • Pre-employment training, matched to your business sector and delivered by you or a local training provider
  • A short work placement with your business
  • A guaranteed job interview or help with your application process

Courses are run in a range of sectors including construction, security, teaching assistant, hospitality and health and social care.
