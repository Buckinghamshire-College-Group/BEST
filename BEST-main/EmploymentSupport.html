Employment Support

Oasis Partnership | Routes to Work

We help individuals facing disadvantage build brighter futures through tailored training, career guidance, and community support.

  • The Routes to Work programme offers 1:1 and group support for anyone 16+ in Buckinghamshire who is unemployed or seeking new skills. Services include:
  • In-house & external training
  • CV writing & interview prep


Fedcap | RESTART Programme

Fedcap's Restart provides employability support to participants referred from DWP, withi High Wycombe and the south of Buckinghamshire.


TCHC | RESTART Programme

Working in partnership with Jobs 22 at TCHC Restart we provide employability support to participants referred from DWP, within Aylesbury Vale and the north of Buckinghamshire.


BUDS | Buckinghamshire Disability Service

Reach4Work is delivered by the whole BuDS community, to support all disabled people into or closer to work. Advisors support the Reach4work Participants through progression, workshops as well as hands on work experience.


Talkback | Supported Internships

Talkback’s internships offer real-world experience with flexible 6, 9, or 12-month options. Our flexible approach aims to transition people away from a traditional academic calendar and towards the world of work. The majority of your time is based in the workplace, with additional support to gain qualifications in English, Maths, or a relevant subject. We match placements to your skills across sectors like retail, care, sport, and hospitality—helping you transition confidently into work.


Talkback | Equip Work Experience

Our support through education, life skills development and work experience has the ultimate goal of preparing our members for supported internships, paid employment or volunteer work in the future. Members can continue to one of our job clubs and receive ongoing workplace support once they have secured a job if they receive the right funding support. Students undertake work experience placements to learn skills required for working life and build the foundations for finding work in the future. From retail, to office work, woodwork skills, mechanics and many others we always try to accommodate their interests to find the right placement for them.


Talkback | Equip Supported Internships

Talkback creates unique pathways for neurodiverse young adults and people with learning disabilities to ensure the positive transition to adulthood and employment or meaningful occupation. Package: Work study programme with majority of week spent in a workplace. Includes: Employability skills Work place job skills development and support Advanced travel training Options for further qualifications - English and Maths Progression: Sustained paid employment


Talkback | Personal Development and Skills - Work Experience

Our work experience groups help you build practical skills in retail, hospitality, and customer-facing roles. Each programme blends hands-on experience with theory sessions to support your next steps, whether into volunteering or paid work. The Accessible Workshop creates realistic progression pathways.

Through partnerships with The Food Hub and The Mustard Seed Café in High Wycombe, participants gain skills in:

  • Confidence in speaking to people
  • Customer service and communication
  • Functional maths through pricing
  • Using a till and handling transactions
  • Reception duties and phone handling
  • Retail experience in a clothing shop
Our Accessible Workshop covers a wide range of woodworking and practical skills. It creates realistic progression pathways to work or volunteer.


Talkback | Supported Employment

We help autistic individuals and people with learning disabilities find and thrive in paid employment. Our approach ensures everyone feels valued, respected, and supported in the workplace

Our 3-Step Programme:

1. Getting Work Ready
  • Confidence & team building
  • Understanding job skills & workplace culture
  • Employer visits & travel training
  • Independent living skills (e.g. cooking, budgeting)
2. Finding a Role
  • Supported internship
  • Career advice & job clubs
  • CV writing & LinkedIn support
  • Job search & interview prep
3. Ongoing Support
  • Dedicated job coaches
  • Workplace adjustments
  • Confidence building & role navigation
  • Support with co-worker relationship
