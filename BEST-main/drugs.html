Drug Addiction and Alcohol Support


Seek Drug Addiction Support

If you need treatment for drug addiction, you're entitled to NHS care in the same way as anyone else who has a health problem. With the right help and support, it's possible for you to get drug free and stay that way.

Alcohol Dependency Support

Realising you have a problem with alcohol is the first big step to getting help.

  • you often feel the need to have a drink
  • you get into trouble because of your drinking
  • other people warn you about how much you're drinking
  • you think your drinking is causing you problems
One Recovery Bucks

One Recovery Bucks provides a confidential, free and easy-to-acess service to anyone living in Buckinghamshire impacted by their own or someone else's use of alcohol and drugs.
