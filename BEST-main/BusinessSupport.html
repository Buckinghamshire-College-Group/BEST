Buckinghamshire Business First | Support for Starting a Business

Buckinghamshire Business First provides comprehensive online support for those looking to start a business, plus start up membership for ongoing support and help.


Buckinghamshire Business First | Workforce Skills

1:1 Advice and support for businesses to help them develop their workforce. Services provided include: support with recruitment, developing a People Plan, advice on training and development, support regarding apprenticeships and work placements, advice on compliance with employment law, help in becoming a disability confident employer, information and guidance on wellbeing at work, and engaging with schools and colleges
